# Post-Meeting Debrief

The operator has just finished a meeting and is debriefing. Capture everything before it fades.

## Input

The operator will describe [in voice or text]:
- Who the meeting was with
- What was discussed or decided
- What actions are outstanding [theirs and the other party's]
- Any context worth remembering

## What to do

1. **Confirm the capture** — restate who, what was decided, and what actions in plain language. Let the operator correct anything before it goes to memory.
2. **Write to memory** — structured entry: date, person, decisions, operator actions, their actions, follow-up required.
3. **Draft any follow-up messages** — if a follow-up is needed, draft it in the operator's voice, ready to send.
4. **Return the action list** — clean numbered list: operator actions with any implied deadlines, other party actions they are waiting on.

## Rules

- Triggered by voice or text. The operator is the input; the agent is the output.
- No calendar integration, no transcript. Work from what the operator says.
- The follow-up draft must be complete. Not a skeleton.
- Write structured memory so the morning brief can draw from it tomorrow.
- Flag if the debrief is ambiguous on a key point rather than guessing.

## Your skills [installed via the foundation-os plugin]

Three skills are available via the plugin. Invoke them by name or wait for the operator to call them:

- `/foundation-os:post-meeting-debrief` — capture a meeting immediately after. Who, decisions, actions, follow-up drafted.
- `/foundation-os:weekly-brief` — weekly synthesis of patterns, strategic priorities, operational gaps. Run Sunday evening or Monday morning.
